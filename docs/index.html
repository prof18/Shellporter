How it works

1

Focus your IDE

Work in JetBrains, VS Code, Cursor, Xcode, or Antigravity.

→
2

Press the hotkey

^⌥⌘T to open a terminal at the project root.

→
3

You're there

Your terminal opens, already cd'd into the right directory.

Features

>_

5+ IDEs supported

JetBrains (IntelliJ, Android Studio, Fleet, …), VS Code, Cursor, Xcode, and Antigravity. Resolved via accessibility APIs and window metadata.

▦

5 terminals

Terminal.app, iTerm2, Ghostty, Kitty, and a custom command option for anything else.

⌘

Global hotkeys

^⌥⌘T to open the terminal, ^⌥⌘C to copy the path. Works from any app.

◑

Menu bar only

Lives in your menu bar. No Dock icon, no window clutter. Just click or press the hotkey.

⟳

Smart project detection

Automatically figures out which project is open in your IDE. Remembers recent projects so repeat launches are instant.

◇

Privacy-first

Fully local. No network calls, no telemetry, no analytics. Your project paths stay on your machine.
